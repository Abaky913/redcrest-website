Our Story

Started in a Kitchen.
Stayed That Way.

Redcrest Foods began when we got tired of buying sauce that tasted like a chemistry experiment. We wanted something real — made with actual tomatoes, actual garlic, and actual care.

What started as batches for friends and family grew into something bigger when word spread that this was sauce that actually tasted of something. We've been handcrafting every jar since 2020, and we have no plans to change how we do it.
